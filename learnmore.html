NBA Play Off Bet

whitepaper

what to bet?

1, NBA Playoff Eastern Final. 2, NBA PlayOff Western Final. 3, NBA PlayOff Final Final. who will be champion of each match?

what is the algorithm to calculate bonus?

Let take the match:Eastern Final as example:

let A = amount of your betting on team who win champion.

let T = amount of all betting on team who win champion.

let O = amount of all betting on the match

your bonus = A * O / T

Is it totally free?

To interact with contract, you have to pay GAS to Qtum miners. The contract only set 1% donation for WINNER only. and it is not mandatory, you are appreciated to donate.if you really don't want to donate, that is fine, you can operate contract interface:setDonationPercent to adjust it. for the sake of programming easy, your inputed percentage will be substracted 1 as your final donation percentage. and, please NOTE, you have to pay GAS for that, we encourage you to donate that GAS to help us better

when to start?

Every season, once final 4 teams are finalized, the contract start working

when to stop betting?

Champion have to win 4 of 7 games, so either of team win 3 games, stop betting.

how is BTT rewarding?

BTT is BiTrust token based on Qtum, it will be rewarded to any participant of the contract, the rate is:1Qtum get 1 BTT reward. if you finally win, your total bonus will be rewarded again with same rate.
